Nomadic Matt

Nomadic Matt's site is well organzied, graphic, interactive, and flows perfectly. The layout and positioning of the content makes it look quite nice. The bad, it's a bit cluttered. I recognize html links, headers, paragraphs, lists, divs, forms, images, css, and nav bar. There appears to be a bunch of javascript that I'm not familiar with and have no idea what it's doing. The amount of code is overwhelming to look at!
